---

## Top-Line Recommendation Criteria

| Recommendation | When to Use |
|---------------|-------------|
| **ACCEPTABLE AS DRAFTED** | No HIGH severity issues; all 9 Protective Requirements met or substantially met; no compliance concerns |
| **NEGOTIATE BEFORE SIGNING** | One or more HIGH severity issues that can be resolved through redlining; or multiple MEDIUM issues creating cumulative risk |
| **ESCALATE — DO NOT SIGN** | Deal-breaker issues present (perpetual irrevocable rights, pay-for-play structure, eligibility risk, no compensation, unlimited liability); requires senior attorney review or deal restructuring |
