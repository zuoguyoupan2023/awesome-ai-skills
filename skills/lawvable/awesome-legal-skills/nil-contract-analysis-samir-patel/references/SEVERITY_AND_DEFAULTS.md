# Severity Ratings and Ownership Defaults

## Severity Ratings

| Rating | Criteria | Example |
|--------|----------|---------|
| **HIGH** | Immediate legal risk, eligibility impact, or significant financial exposure | Perpetual IP grant, no compensation, pay-for-play structure |
| **MEDIUM** | Unfavorable terms that should be negotiated but are not deal-breakers | Overbroad exclusivity, distant venue, one-sided moral clause |
| **LOW** | Minor issues or missing best practices | No severability clause, missing force majeure, no electronic signature clause |

## Ownership & Deadline Defaults

If the user does not specify, use these defaults in the issue log:

| Issue Type | Default Owner | Default Deadline |
|------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Redline / clause revision | Legal | Before execution |
| Compliance / eligibility question | Compliance + Legal | Before execution |
| Business term (compensation, exclusivity) | Business + Legal | Before execution |
| Institutional disclosure | Compliance | Within 48 hours of execution |
| Agent registration verification | Legal | Before execution |
