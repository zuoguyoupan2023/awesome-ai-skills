# Brand Ideation Output Template

A fillable template for the ideation deliverable. Captures the candidate names, positioning territories, mood directions, and narrative angles produced during ideation.

The deliverable is not a single answer. It is a structured set of options the team can evaluate, narrow, and combine.

---

## Project metadata

**Brand / project:** [Name]
**Ideation lead:** [Name]
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Inputs used:** [Discovery report, brief, prior decks - link them]

## Recap: what we are ideating against

Two to four sentences. Pull from the discovery report. Anyone reading this deliverable cold should know:

- Who the audience is
- What position the brand is trying to take
- What the constraints are (legal, geographic, category)
