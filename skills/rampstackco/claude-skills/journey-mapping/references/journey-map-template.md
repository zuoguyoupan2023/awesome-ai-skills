## Re-mapping schedule

Journey maps decay. Schedule re-mapping:

- **Triggering events:** Major product launch, new persona segment, significant business model change, drop in NPS or retention
- **Default cadence:** Annual review, with light updates quarterly

## Companion artifacts

Often produced alongside a journey map:

- **Persona document.** Detailed persona that this journey map represents.
- **Quote library.** Direct user quotes organized by phase. Powerful in stakeholder presentations.
- **Highlight reel video.** 5 to 10 minute compilation of user moments mapped to journey phases.
- **Service blueprint.** Detailed back-stage map (this template includes a layer; a full blueprint deserves its own document).
- **Opportunity backlog.** All opportunities, scored and queued for execution.
