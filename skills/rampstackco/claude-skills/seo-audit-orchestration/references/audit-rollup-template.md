# Audit rollup template

A template for the final SEO audit deliverable. Adapt sections to fit the audit goal and stakeholder appetite.

---

## Document structure

```
1. Executive summary           (1 page)
2. Audit charter               (1 page)
3. Themes and recommendations  (5-7 themes, 1 page each)
4. 90-day roadmap              (1-2 pages)
5. Sub-audit appendix          (linked or attached)
6. Methodology notes           (1 page)
```

Total: 15-30 pages. Executive summary always readable in 5 minutes.

---

## 1. Executive summary template

```
# [Property] SEO Audit

**Audit date:** [date]
**Period covered:** [date range]
**Auditor:** [name or team]

## Headline

[One sentence. The state of SEO at this property.]

## Top 3 themes

1. **[Theme name].** [One sentence. Why it matters.]
2. **[Theme name].** [One sentence. Why it matters.]
3. **[Theme name].** [One sentence. Why it matters.]

## Top 3 quick wins

1. **[Action].** Estimated impact: [target metric movement]. Effort: [size].
2. **[Action].** Estimated impact: [target metric movement]. Effort: [size].
3. **[Action].** Estimated impact: [target metric movement]. Effort: [size].

## Recommended next 90 days

[3-5 bullets describing the proposed plan.]

## What is going well

[2-4 bullets. Avoid an audit that reads as 100% negative.]
```

---

## Style guidance

- Lead with the "so what." Stakeholders skim.
- Quantify wherever possible. Vague impact claims get ignored.
- Pair every problem with a proposed fix. Audits without recommendations are reports.
- Keep technical jargon out of the executive summary.
- Use tables and bullets. Avoid wall-of-text.

---

## Common length targets by audit type

| Audit type | Total length | Executive summary | Themes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Quarterly health check | 10-15 pages | 1 page | 3-4 themes |
| Full annual audit | 25-40 pages | 1-2 pages | 5-7 themes |
| Pre-acquisition due diligence | 30-50 pages | 2 pages | 6-10 themes |
| Post-migration verification | 10-20 pages | 1 page | 3-5 themes |

---

## Pre-delivery checklist

- [ ] Executive summary readable in 5 minutes
- [ ] Every theme tied to a quantified impact
- [ ] Every recommendation tied to an owner
- [ ] Quick wins called out separately
- [ ] 90-day roadmap is sequenced and resourced
- [ ] Methodology and caveats are documented
- [ ] Internal review completed before stakeholder readout
- [ ] Next audit date scheduled
