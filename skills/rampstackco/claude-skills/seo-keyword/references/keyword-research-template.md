## Refresh schedule

This research should be revisited:
- **Quarterly** for fast-moving topics (tech, news, trends, finance)
- **Annually** for evergreen topics
- **After any major SERP feature change** (e.g., AI overviews appearing on key queries)
- **After authority increases** that unlock harder keywords (e.g., domain rating going up by 10+ points)
