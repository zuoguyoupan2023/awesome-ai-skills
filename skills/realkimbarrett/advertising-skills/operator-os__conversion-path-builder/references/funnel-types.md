# Funnel Path Selection (What Converts Best)

## The Core Idea

Not all traffic should go through the same funnel.

The right path depends on:

* awareness level
* price point
* urgency
* trust required

---

## Core Funnel Types

### 1. Direct Call Funnel

Ad → Booking Page → Call

**Use When:**

* High intent audience
* Strong offer
* Clear problem

---

### 2. Lead Magnet Funnel

Ad → Opt-in → Nurture → Call

**Use When:**

* Lower awareness
* Longer sales cycle
* Need to build trust

---

### 3. VSL Funnel

Ad → VSL → Call / Purchase

**Use When:**

* Complex offer
* Needs explanation
* High ticket

---

### 4. Quiz / Application Funnel

Ad → Quiz → Call

**Use When:**

* Qualification is important
* Personalisation helps
* You want higher quality leads

---

## Decision Logic

Ask:

1. Do they know they need this?
2. Do they trust someone like you?
3. Is the outcome obvious?

If YES → shorten funnel
If NO → add steps

---

## The Real Insight

Most funnels fail because:

They are too long for high-intent buyers
or
too short for low-awareness buyers

---

## Rule

Match funnel length to trust gap.
